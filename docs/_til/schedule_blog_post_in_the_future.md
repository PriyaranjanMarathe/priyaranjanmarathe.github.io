Learnt a cool Jekyll trick to schedule a blog post in the future.

1. Set a date in the properties header for the page to a future date.

   ```
   ---
   layout: "post"
   title: "Future post"
   date: "2024-11-01 00:00:00 -0600"
   categories: "jekyll update"
   ---

   ```
